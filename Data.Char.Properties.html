char-is-discreteⁱ : is-discreteⁱ Char
char-is-discreteⁱ x y with ℕ-is-discreteⁱ (to-ℕ x) (to-ℕ y)
... | yes p = yes $ char-to-nat-injectiveⁱ _ _ p
... | no ¬p = no λ p → ¬p (apⁱ to-ℕ p)
